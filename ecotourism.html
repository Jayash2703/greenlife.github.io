Grassland Ecosystem

"Nearly 50% of grasslands have experienced some form of degradation be it conversion, invasive species, overgrazing, climate change or altered fire regimes, the conservationist told us. "Grassland conservation is of global concern and everyone needs to be working together to solve this problem to the benefit of climate change, biodiversity, other ecosystem services, and the livelihoods that depend on grasslands."

The grass is not greener on the other side, it's greener where you water it. Grasslands are one of the World's most important ecosystems owing to the vast amount of carbon sequestration that takes place through them and the amazing plethora of biodiversity that they support. Desert Ecosystem

Dry sands stretching for several thousands of miles. Try to hold it in the palm of your hand and it flies away with the hot wind. There’s nothing in the skies except the blazing sun. The environment is so harsh for humans that our minds play games with us showing us mirages. How is it possible that hundreds of animal and plant species can exist and thrive in such a harsh environment?
